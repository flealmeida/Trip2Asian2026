# Your Day Plan

## 1. Ruins of St. Paul's & Monte Fort
Time: 09:00
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Explore the iconic facade of the Ruins of St. Paul's and ascend to Monte Fort for panoramic views of Macau's historic center. These adjacent UNESCO World Heritage sites offer a glimpse into Macau's colonial past.

### Travel to Senado Square
Method: walking
Time: 15 minutes

## 2. Senado Square
Time: 10:45
Duration: 1 hour 15 minutes

Immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of Senado Square, a UNESCO World Heritage site with charming Portuguese colonial architecture, mosaic pavements, and bustling shops.

### Travel to A-Ma Temple
Method: walking
Time: 15 minutes

## 3. A-Ma Temple
Time: 12:15
Duration: 1 hour

Visit the oldest temple in Macau, dedicated to the goddess Mazu, protector of seafarers and fishermen. Experience the rich cultural and spiritual heritage at this serene temple.

### Travel to Macau Tower
Method: taxi
Time: 15 minutes

## 4. Macau Tower
Time: 13:35
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Ascend to the observation deck of Macau Tower for breathtaking panoramic views of the city, Pearl River Delta, and beyond. For the adventurous, consider the bungee jump or skywalk.

### Travel to The Venetian Macao
Method: taxi
Time: 20 minutes

## 5. The Venetian Macao
Time: 15:25
Duration: 5 hours 5 minutes

Experience the grandeur of The Venetian Macao, a luxurious resort with an impressive casino, expansive shopping arcade with gondola rides, and world-class entertainment. Enjoy dinner and explore this magnificent property.
