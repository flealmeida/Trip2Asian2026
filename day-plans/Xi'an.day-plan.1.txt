# Your Day Plan

## 1. Depart from Xi'an Bell Tower
Time: 08:30
Duration: 0 minutes

Start your day trip to the Terracotta Army from the historic center of Xi'an, near the iconic Bell Tower.

## 2. Terracotta Army Museum (Museum of Qin Terra-cotta Warriors and Horses)
Time: 09:30
Duration: 3 hours

Explore the awe-inspiring collection of life-sized terracotta warriors, horses, and chariots, guarding the tomb of Emperor Qin Shi Huang. This UNESCO World Heritage site is an archaeological marvel.

## 3. Lunch Break
Time: 12:30
Duration: 45 minutes

Enjoy a well-deserved lunch near the Terracotta Army complex, offering a variety of local Shaanxi cuisine.

## 4. Emperor Qinshihuang's Mausoleum Site Park
Time: 13:30
Duration: 2 hours

Visit the burial mound of the first Emperor of China, Qin Shi Huang. While the actual tomb remains unopened, the site offers insights into the vast imperial complex and its historical significance, complementing your visit to the Terracotta Army.

## 5. Arrive back in Xi'an City Center
Time: 16:45
Duration: 0 minutes

Conclude your day trip back in the heart of Xi'an, ready to reflect on the historical wonders you've witnessed.
