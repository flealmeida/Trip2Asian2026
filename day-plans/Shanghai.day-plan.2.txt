# Your Day Plan

## 1. Fuxing Park
Time: 09:00
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Start your day with a leisurely stroll through Fuxing Park, a charming example of a French-style park where you can observe locals practicing tai chi, dancing, and playing mahjong in the morning.

### Travel to Sinan Mansions
Method: walking
Time: 15 minutes

## 2. Sinan Mansions
Time: 10:45
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Explore the historic Sinan Mansions area, a collection of beautifully preserved European-style villas, many of which now house upscale restaurants, cafes, and boutiques. It offers a glimpse into Shanghai's past elegance.

### Travel to Tianzifang
Method: walking
Time: 15 minutes

## 3. Tianzifang
Time: 12:25
Duration: 2 hours 30 minutes

Wander through the labyrinthine alleys of Tianzifang, a bohemian district filled with art studios, craft shops, cafes, and restaurants housed in renovated traditional "shikumen" buildings. This is an excellent spot for lunch and some unique souvenir shopping.

### Travel to Wukang Road & Wukang Mansion
Method: walking
Time: 10 minutes

## 4. Wukang Road & Wukang Mansion
Time: 15:25
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Take a leisurely walk along Wukang Road, renowned for its diverse architectural styles and historic residences. Admire the iconic Wukang Mansion, a unique ship-like building at the intersection, and the charming tree-lined avenue.

### Travel to Hengshan Road
Method: walking
Time: 30 minutes

## 5. Hengshan Road
Time: 17:05
Duration: 2 hours

Conclude your day on Hengshan Road, known for its vibrant evening atmosphere, European-style architecture, and numerous restaurants and bars. Enjoy dinner and soak in the lively ambiance.
