# Your Day Plan

## 1. Tung Chung Cable Car Terminal
Time: 09:00
Duration: 45 minutes

Start your day by taking the Ngong Ping 360 cable car from Tung Chung to Ngong Ping Village. Enjoy panoramic views of Lantau Island and the South China Sea during the ride.

## 2. Ngong Ping Village
Time: 09:45
Duration: 45 minutes

Explore the cultural themed village with various shops and restaurants. This is the gateway to the Big Buddha and Po Lin Monastery.

## 3. Tian Tan Buddha (Big Buddha)
Time: 10:30
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Ascend the 268 steps to visit the magnificent bronze statue of Tian Tan Buddha, offering serene views of the surrounding mountains.

## 4. Po Lin Monastery
Time: 12:00
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Visit the grand Buddhist monastery next to the Big Buddha. Admire the beautiful temples and enjoy a vegetarian lunch at its famous restaurant.

## 5. Tai O Fishing Village
Time: 14:00
Duration: 2 hours 30 minutes

Discover the "Venice of Hong Kong," a charming stilt house village. Take a boat ride to spot pink dolphins (seasonal) and explore local markets.

## 6. Citygate Outlets
Time: 17:15
Duration: 1 hour 45 minutes

Conclude your day with some shopping at Citygate Outlets, located conveniently near the Tung Chung MTR station, before heading back.
