# Your Day Plan

## 1. Gyeongbokgung Palace
Time: 09:00
Duration: 2 hours 30 minutes

Start your historical journey at the grandest of Seoul's five palaces, the primary royal palace of the Joseon Dynasty. Witness the changing of the guard ceremony (check times) and explore its majestic grounds, including Geunjeongjeon Hall and Gyeonghoeru Pavilion.

## 2. National Folk Museum of Korea
Time: 11:30
Duration: 1 hour

Located within the Gyeongbokgung grounds, this museum offers a deep dive into the daily lives and culture of the Korean people from prehistoric times to the modern era, with extensive exhibits on traditional clothing, food, and housing.

### Travel to Bukchon Hanok Village
Method: walking
Time: 15 minutes

## 3. Bukchon Hanok Village
Time: 12:45
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Wander through this charming traditional Korean village nestled between Gyeongbokgung and Changdeokgung Palaces. Admire the beautifully preserved hanok (traditional houses) and enjoy panoramic views of the city, experiencing a glimpse into Seoul's past.

### Travel to Changdeokgung Palace & Huwon (Secret Garden)
Method: walking
Time: 15 minutes

## 4. Changdeokgung Palace & Huwon (Secret Garden)
Time: 14:30
Duration: 2 hours 30 minutes

A UNESCO World Heritage site, this palace is renowned for its architectural harmony with its natural surroundings. Don't miss the tranquil Secret Garden (Huwon), a masterpiece of Korean landscaping (note: Huwon often requires a separate, timed tour).
