# Your Day Plan

## 1. Nanluoguxiang Hutong
Time: 09:00
Duration: 2 hours

Start your day exploring one of Beijing's most famous and best-preserved hutongs. Wander through its narrow alleys, admire traditional courtyard homes, browse unique boutiques, and enjoy the lively atmosphere.

## 2. Shichahai Scenic Area (Houhai Lake & Yandaixiejie)
Time: 11:30
Duration: 2 hours

A picturesque area comprising three lakes (Qianhai, Houhai, and Xihai) surrounded by historic residences, temples, and lively bars. Walk along Houhai Lake, visit the ancient Yandaixiejie (Tobacco Pouch Street), and soak in the serene beauty.

## 3. Lunch & Bell and Drum Towers Area
Time: 13:45
Duration: 1.5 hours

Enjoy a traditional Beijing lunch at a local eatery in the hutongs surrounding the iconic Bell and Drum Towers. Afterward, explore these impressive historical structures and the charming, less commercialized alleys nearby.

## 4. Dashilar Street & Qianmen Main Street
Time: 16:00
Duration: 2 hours

Experience the vibrant commercial history of Beijing at Dashilar, a bustling traditional street known for its century-old shops, theaters, and local snacks. It's just off the grand Qianmen Main Street, offering a glimpse into old Beijing's merchant culture.
