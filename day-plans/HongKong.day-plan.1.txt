# Your Day Plan

## 1. Central District (Morning Exploration)
Time: 09:00
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Begin your day in the bustling heart of Hong Kong's financial and commercial district. Explore iconic skyscrapers and historical colonial buildings like the Former French Mission Building or St. John's Cathedral.

## 2. Victoria Peak
Time: 10:50
Duration: 2 hours

Ascend to Victoria Peak via the historic Peak Tram for breathtaking panoramic views of Hong Kong Island, Kowloon, and Victoria Harbour. Enjoy the Sky Terrace 428 and explore the shops and attractions at the Peak Tower.

## 3. Lunch in Central
Time: 13:20
Duration: 1 hour

Enjoy a delicious lunch in Central, offering a wide array of dining options from local Cantonese fare in a traditional cha chaan teng to international cuisine in a modern restaurant.

## 4. Star Ferry Ride
Time: 14:35
Duration: 1 hour

Experience the iconic Star Ferry crossing Victoria Harbour. This scenic ride offers stunning views of both Hong Kong Island and the Tsim Sha Tsui skyline, a true Hong Kong classic.

## 5. Mid-Levels Escalator & SoHo Exploration
Time: 15:50
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Explore the fascinating Mid-Levels Escalator system, the longest outdoor covered escalator system in the world. Discover the vibrant streets of SoHo and its trendy boutiques, art galleries, and historic alleyways.

### Travel to Dinner in SoHo/Central
Method: walking
Time: 15 minutes

## 6. Dinner in SoHo/Central
Time: 17:35
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Conclude your day with dinner in the lively SoHo district, known for its diverse culinary scene ranging from casual eateries to upscale restaurants, or find a spot in the broader Central area.
