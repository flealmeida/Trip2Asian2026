# Your Day Plan

## 1. Shanghai Museum
Time: 09:00
Duration: 2 hours 30 minutes

Begin your day exploring ancient Chinese art and artifacts at the Shanghai Museum, located in People's Square. It houses a vast collection of bronze, ceramics, calligraphy, and more, offering a deep dive into China's rich history.

### Travel to Shanghai Urban Planning Exhibition Center
Method: walking
Time: 15 minutes

## 2. Shanghai Urban Planning Exhibition Center
Time: 11:45
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Discover the past, present, and future of Shanghai's urban development through detailed models, interactive exhibits, and a massive scale model of the city. It provides a fascinating insight into modern China's rapid growth.

### Travel to The Bund
Method: walking
Time: 15 minutes

## 3. The Bund
Time: 14:00
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Stroll along the iconic waterfront promenade, offering breathtaking views of Shanghai's colonial-era buildings on one side and the futuristic Lujiazui skyline on the other. It's a perfect spot to appreciate the blend of old and new Shanghai.

### Travel to Oriental Pearl TV Tower & Lujiazui Area
Method: public transit
Time: 20 minutes

## 4. Oriental Pearl TV Tower & Lujiazui Area
Time: 16:00
Duration: 2 hours

Experience modern China with a visit to the Lujiazui financial district. You can choose to go up the iconic Oriental Pearl TV Tower for panoramic views, or simply enjoy the futuristic architecture and vibrant atmosphere of the area. There are also many shopping and dining options here.

### Travel to Dinner in Lujiazui
Method: walking
Time: 10 minutes

## 5. Dinner in Lujiazui
Time: 18:10
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Enjoy a modern Chinese dinner at one of the many upscale restaurants in the Lujiazui area, offering spectacular views of the illuminated skyline to conclude your day.
