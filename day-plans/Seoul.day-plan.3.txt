# Your Day Plan

## 1. Gwangjang Market
Time: 09:00
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Start your day with a traditional Korean breakfast and explore one of Korea's oldest and largest traditional markets, famous for its street food like bindaetteok (mung bean pancakes), mayak gimbap, and tteokbokki.

### Travel to Bukchon Hanok Village
Method: public transit
Time: 20 minutes

## 2. Bukchon Hanok Village
Time: 11:30
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Wander through this traditional Korean village with beautifully preserved hanok houses, offering a glimpse into Seoul's past and a peaceful atmosphere after your market visit.

### Travel to Insadong
Method: public transit
Time: 20 minutes

## 3. Insadong
Time: 14:00
Duration: 2 hours

Immerse yourself in Korean culture in Insadong, a vibrant neighborhood known for its traditional tea houses, art galleries, antique shops, and street performances. Enjoy a traditional tea or snack.

### Travel to Dongdaemun Design Plaza (DDP) & Night Market
Method: walking
Time: 15 minutes

## 4. Dongdaemun Design Plaza (DDP) & Night Market
Time: 18:00
Duration: 3 hours

Marvel at the futuristic architecture of DDP. As evening falls, dive into the bustling Dongdaemun Night Market, famous for its fashion, street food, and late-night shopping. Enjoy dinner and soak in the lively atmosphere.
