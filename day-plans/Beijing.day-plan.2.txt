# Your Day Plan

## 1. Departure from Beijing
Time: 08:00
Duration: 30 minutes

Start your day trip from a central location in Beijing. Ensure you have comfortable shoes and water for the day.

### Travel to Mutianyu Great Wall
Method: driving
Time: 1 hour 30 minutes

## 2. Mutianyu Great Wall
Time: 09:30
Duration: 4 hours

Explore the well-preserved Mutianyu section of the Great Wall. Enjoy breathtaking views, walk along the ancient battlements, and consider taking the cable car up and the toboggan slide down for a fun experience. This section is less crowded than Badaling and offers excellent photo opportunities.

### Travel to Lunch near Mutianyu
Method: driving
Time: 1 hour 30 minutes

## 3. Lunch near Mutianyu
Time: 13:45
Duration: 1 hour

Enjoy a well-deserved lunch at one of the local restaurants near the Mutianyu Great Wall. Try some authentic local Chinese cuisine.

## 4. Arrival in Beijing
Time: 16:15
Duration: 30 minutes

Conclude your Great Wall day trip back in central Beijing.
