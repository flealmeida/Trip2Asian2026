# Your Day Plan

## 1. Ladies' Market
Time: 09:00
Duration: 2 hours

Explore one of Hong Kong's most famous street markets, offering a wide array of clothing, accessories, souvenirs, and electronics. Practice your bargaining skills!

## 2. Lunch in Mong Kok
Time: 11:15
Duration: 1 hour

Enjoy authentic local Cantonese cuisine in one of the many eateries around Mong Kok. Try dim sum, congee, or roasted meats.

## 3. Tsim Sha Tsui Promenade & Avenue of Stars
Time: 12:45
Duration: 3 hours

Stroll along the iconic waterfront promenade, offering stunning views of Victoria Harbour and the Hong Kong Island skyline. Visit the Avenue of Stars to see tributes to Hong Kong's film industry.

## 4. Dinner in Tsim Sha Tsui
Time: 16:45
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Dine at one of the many restaurants in Tsim Sha Tsui, ranging from casual local eateries to upscale international dining, with options for harbour views.

## 5. Symphony of Lights Viewing
Time: 18:30
Duration: 1 hour

Experience the spectacular nightly multimedia show, "A Symphony of Lights," featuring synchronized building lights, laser beams, and music across Victoria Harbour. Find a good spot along the Tsim Sha Tsui Promenade.
