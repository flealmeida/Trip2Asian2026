# Your Day Plan

## 1. East Palace Gate
Time: 09:00
Duration: 30 minutes

The main entrance to the Summer Palace, where you can start your exploration. Admire the traditional imperial architecture before heading deeper into the palace grounds.

### Travel to Hall of Benevolence and Longevity
Method: walking
Time: 10 minutes

## 2. Hall of Benevolence and Longevity
Time: 09:40
Duration: 45 minutes

An important administrative area where Emperor Guangxu and Empress Dowager Cixi conducted court affairs. Marvel at the intricate designs and historical artifacts.

### Travel to Garden of Virtue and Harmony (Grand Theatre)
Method: walking
Time: 10 minutes

## 3. Garden of Virtue and Harmony (Grand Theatre)
Time: 10:35
Duration: 45 minutes

Home to the magnificent Grand Theatre, where Empress Dowager Cixi enjoyed Peking Opera performances. The three-story stage is an architectural marvel.

### Travel to Long Corridor
Method: walking
Time: 10 minutes

## 4. Long Corridor
Time: 11:30
Duration: 1 hour 15 minutes

A UNESCO World Heritage site, this 728-meter long covered walkway is adorned with over 14,000 paintings depicting classical Chinese landscapes and stories. It offers stunning views of Kunming Lake.

### Travel to Marble Boat
Method: walking
Time: 10 minutes

## 5. Marble Boat
Time: 13:45
Duration: 45 minutes

A unique and elegant two-story boat carved from marble, built by Empress Dowager Cixi with funds originally intended for the navy. It symbolizes stability and longevity.

### Travel to Tower of Buddhist Incense (Foxiangge)
Method: walking
Time: 10 minutes

## 6. Tower of Buddhist Incense (Foxiangge)
Time: 14:45
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

The magnificent Tower of Buddhist Incense is the most prominent structure on Longevity Hill, offering panoramic views of the entire Summer Palace and Kunming Lake. It was a place for Empress Dowager Cixi to offer incense to Buddha.

### Travel to Kunming Lake
Method: walking
Time: 15 minutes

## 7. Kunming Lake
Time: 16:30
Duration: 1 hour

Spend time by the expansive Kunming Lake, the central feature of the Summer Palace. Enjoy a relaxing boat ride or a leisurely stroll along its shores, taking in the tranquil scenery.

### Travel to Seventeen-Arch Bridge
Method: walking
Time: 15 minutes

## 8. Seventeen-Arch Bridge
Time: 17:45
Duration: 45 minutes

A beautiful and iconic bridge connecting Kunming Lake to Nanhu Island. Admire its intricate carvings and the stunning reflections on the water, especially during sunset.
