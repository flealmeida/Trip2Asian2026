# Your Day Plan

## 1. Temple of Heaven
Time: 08:30
Duration: 2 hours 30 minutes

Start the extra Beijing day at the Temple of Heaven, one of the city's most beautiful imperial sites. Arrive early for cooler weather, fewer crowds, and a chance to see locals exercising in the surrounding park before exploring the Hall of Prayer for Good Harvests and the Circular Mound Altar.

## 2. Lunch near Yonghegong
Time: 11:45
Duration: 1 hour

Head north for lunch around Yonghegong and Guozijian Street, an easy area for noodles, dumplings, or a quieter cafe break before the afternoon.

## 3. Lama Temple (Yonghegong)
Time: 13:00
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Visit Beijing's most famous Tibetan Buddhist temple, known for its richly decorated halls, incense-filled courtyards, and towering sandalwood Maitreya statue.

## 4. Confucius Temple & Guozijian
Time: 14:45
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Walk a few minutes west to the Confucius Temple and Imperial Academy area for a calmer, more scholarly side of old Beijing. The courtyards, stone steles, and historic academy buildings make this a great contrast with the imperial grandeur of the Forbidden City.

## 5. Wudaoying Hutong
Time: 16:30
Duration: 1 hour

Finish the day with a relaxed stroll through Wudaoying Hutong, a more low-key and creative lane than Nanluoguxiang. It is a nice spot for coffee, small design shops, and one last slow walk before heading to the airport.

## 6. Head to PKX Airport
Time: 17:45
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Leave for Beijing Daxing International Airport with time for check-in and security before the evening Etihad departure.
