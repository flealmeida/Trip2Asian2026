# Your Day Plan

## 1. Zhujiajiao Ancient Town Entrance
Time: 09:30
Duration: 30 minutes

Arrive at Zhujiajiao, an ancient water town with a history of over 1,700 years. Known for its canals, bridges, and traditional architecture. Begin your exploration here.

### Travel to Fangsheng Bridge
Method: walking
Time: 15 minutes

## 2. Fangsheng Bridge
Time: 10:15
Duration: 45 minutes

Visit Fangsheng Bridge, the largest stone arch bridge in Zhujiajiao, offering panoramic views of the town and its waterways. A great spot for photos.

### Travel to North Street (Bei Dajie) & Canal Boat Ride
Method: walking
Time: 15 minutes

## 3. North Street (Bei Dajie) & Canal Boat Ride
Time: 11:10
Duration: 2 hours

Stroll along North Street, the best-preserved ancient street in Zhujiajiao, lined with traditional shops and eateries. Enjoy a relaxing gondola ride along the canals, offering a unique perspective of the town.

### Travel to Kezhi Garden
Method: walking
Time: 15 minutes

## 4. Kezhi Garden
Time: 13:25
Duration: 1 hour

Explore Kezhi Garden, a large traditional Chinese garden combining classical and Western architectural styles, featuring beautiful pavilions, ponds, and rockeries.

### Travel to Zhujiajiao Departure Point
Method: walking
Time: 15 minutes

## 5. Zhujiajiao Departure Point
Time: 14:40
Duration: 30 minutes

Conclude your visit to Zhujiajiao and head back to the main entrance area for your return journey to Shanghai.
