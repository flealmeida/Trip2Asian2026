# Your Day Plan

## 1. Hongdae Street Art & Shopping Streets
Time: 09:00
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Start your day exploring the vibrant street art, independent fashion boutiques, and lively atmosphere of Hongdae, known for its youthful energy.

## 2. Coffee Break at a Hongdae Cafe
Time: 10:30
Duration: 1 hour

Enjoy a relaxing coffee and soak in the local ambiance at one of Hongdae's trendy cafes, particularly around the Yeonnam-dong area.

## 3. Lunch & Gangnam Station Area Exploration
Time: 12:10
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Arrive in Gangnam, a symbol of modern Seoul. Have lunch at one of the many upscale restaurants near Gangnam Station, then explore the bustling streets and high-rise buildings.

## 4. Starfield COEX Mall & Byeolmadang Library
Time: 13:40
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Visit the impressive Starfield COEX Mall, home to the iconic Byeolmadang Library, a grand open public library that is a popular photo spot.

## 5. Itaewon Global Village & Shopping
Time: 15:40
Duration: 2 hours

Explore Itaewon, known for its diverse international atmosphere, unique boutiques, and global cuisine. Discover hidden alleys and street art.

## 6. Dinner in Itaewon
Time: 17:40
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Conclude your day with dinner in Itaewon, choosing from a wide array of international restaurants and enjoying the evening ambiance of this lively district.
