# Your Day Plan

## 1. The Bund
Time: 09:00
Duration: 2 hours

Start your day with a stroll along The Bund, a historic waterfront promenade. Marvel at the colonial-era architecture on one side and the futuristic Pudong skyline across the Huangpu River.

## 2. Nanjing Road Pedestrian Street
Time: 11:15
Duration: 1.5 hours

Immerse yourself in the bustling atmosphere of one of the world's busiest shopping streets. Enjoy the diverse array of shops, street food, and historic department stores. Grab lunch here.

## 3. Yu Garden and Old City God Temple
Time: 13:15
Duration: 2 hours

Step back in time to explore the exquisite classical Chinese gardens, rockeries, and pavilions of Yu Garden. Afterwards, wander through the vibrant market surrounding the Old City God Temple, offering traditional snacks and souvenirs.

## 4. Lujiazui Financial District & Skyline
Time: 16:00
Duration: 2.5 hours

Cross the Huangpu River to Pudong and be awed by the towering skyscrapers. Visit an observation deck (e.g., Shanghai Tower, Oriental Pearl TV Tower, Jin Mao Tower) for panoramic views of the city, including The Bund.

## 5. Dinner with Bund View
Time: 18:45
Duration: 1.5 hours

Enjoy a relaxing dinner in Pudong, perhaps at a restaurant offering magnificent night views of The Bund's illuminated colonial buildings across the river, providing a perfect end to your day trip.
