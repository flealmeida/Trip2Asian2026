# Your Day Plan

## 1. Tiananmen Square
Time: 09:00
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Start your day at this iconic public square, a significant site in Chinese history. You can see the Monument to the People's Heroes, the Great Hall of the People, and the National Museum of China (exterior view).

## 2. Tiananmen Gate (Gate of Heavenly Peace)
Time: 10:40
Duration: 30 minutes

Pass through the Gate of Heavenly Peace, famous for its large portrait of Mao Zedong, which serves as the main entrance to the Forbidden City complex.

### Travel to Forbidden City
Method: walking
Time: 10 minutes

## 3. Forbidden City
Time: 11:20
Duration: 4 hours

Explore the vast Forbidden City, a UNESCO World Heritage site and the former imperial palace for 24 emperors. Wander through grand halls, courtyards, and gardens, delving into centuries of Chinese imperial history. Allow ample time to see the Hall of Supreme Harmony, the Imperial Garden, and various palaces.

### Travel to Jingshan Park
Method: walking
Time: 10 minutes

## 4. Jingshan Park
Time: 15:35
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Conclude your day with a visit to Jingshan Park, located directly opposite the Forbidden City's north gate. Climb to the top of the hill for a breathtaking panoramic view of the entire Forbidden City and Beijing's cityscape.
