# Your Day Plan

## 1. Suwon Hwaseong Fortress
Time: 10:00
Duration: 2 hours 30 minutes

A UNESCO World Heritage site, this magnificent fortress wall encircles the city of Suwon. It was built in the late 18th century by King Jeongjo of the Joseon Dynasty. Visitors can walk along the wall, enjoy panoramic views, and explore its various gates, command posts, and pavilions.

## 2. Lunch in Haenggung-dong
Time: 12:45
Duration: 1 hour

Enjoy a traditional Korean meal in the charming Haenggung-dong area, known for its various restaurants and cafes near the fortress.

### Travel to Hwaseong Haenggung Palace
Method: walking
Time: 5 minutes

## 3. Hwaseong Haenggung Palace
Time: 13:50
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

The largest supplemental palace, or 'Haenggung,' used by the kings during the Joseon Dynasty. King Jeongjo often stayed here when visiting Suwon Hwaseong Fortress and held various ceremonies and banquets.

### Travel to Jidong Market
Method: walking
Time: 5 minutes

## 4. Jidong Market
Time: 15:30
Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

One of Suwon's largest traditional markets, offering a vibrant atmosphere, fresh produce, street food, and various goods. It's a great place to experience local culture and find souvenirs.
