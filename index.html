Hong Kong

May 19 – 22 · 4 nights

🇭🇰

Daily Itinerary

1

Hong Kong Island

Morning

Victoria Peak

Take the Peak Tram for panoramic views over the city and harbour. Best in the morning before clouds roll in.

Afternoon

Central District

Explore SoHo, the mid-levels escalator, and colonial-era landmarks. Great street food around the market areas.

Evening

Star Ferry Crossing

Take the iconic Star Ferry from Central to Tsim Sha Tsui for the classic harbour view at dusk.

2

Kowloon

Morning

Temple Street & Markets

Browse street markets in Jordan and Yau Ma Tei for local snacks and souvenirs.

Afternoon

Tsim Sha Tsui Waterfront

The Avenue of Stars, Museum of History, and the famous promenade facing Hong Kong Island.

Evening · 8 PM

Symphony of Lights

World's largest permanent light and sound show, viewable from the Tsim Sha Tsui waterfront.

3

Lantau Island

Morning

Ngong Ping 360 Cable Car

Scenic cable car from Tung Chung with stunning mountain views on the way up to Ngong Ping village.

Mid-Morning

Tian Tan Buddha (Big Buddha)

Climb 268 steps to the world's largest outdoor seated bronze Buddha. Visit Po Lin Monastery below.

Afternoon

Tai O Fishing Village

Charming village on stilts – shrimp paste, boat tours, and a chance to spot pink dolphins.

4

Optional Day

Lamma Island

Car-free island by ferry — seafood restaurants, hiking trails, and a relaxed local vibe.

Option B

Macau Day Trip

1-hour ferry to the "Las Vegas of Asia" — baroque Portuguese architecture, Ruins of St. Paul's, and casinos.

Option C

Lantau Hikes

Sunrise hike to Lantau Peak (934 m) or sections of the Lantau Trail with dramatic coastal views.

Stop 2 of 5

Seoul

May 23 – 27 · 5 nights

🇰🇷

Daily Itinerary

1

Arrival Day

Afternoon

Check-in & Gangnam Explore

Settle into Hotel Anteroom Seoul Gangnam. Explore the upscale Gangnam district — K-drama vibes and bustling streets.

Evening

Garosu-gil Street

Trendy tree-lined boulevard with cafés, boutiques, and some of Seoul's best restaurants for a first dinner.

2

Palaces & History

Morning

Gyeongbokgung Palace

Joseon dynasty's main royal palace. Changing of the Guard at 10 AM and 2 PM. Rent hanbok for free entry!

Afternoon

Bukchon Hanok Village

Winding alleys lined with 600-year-old traditional Korean houses. One of Seoul's most photogenic spots.

Late Afternoon

Insadong & Cheonggyecheon Stream

Artsy street with teashops and crafts, then a walk along the restored urban stream.

3

Neighborhoods

Morning

Hongdae

University district and center of Seoul's indie music/art scene. Street performances, quirky cafés, vintage shops.

Afternoon

Itaewon

International neighborhood with incredible food diversity, Haebangchon hill, and the famous Gyeongnidan-gil.

Evening

N Seoul Tower (Namsan)

Hike or cable car up Namsan for panoramic night views. The iconic "love locks" wall is here.

4

Food & Markets

Morning

Gwangjang Market

Seoul's oldest market — try bindaetteok, mayak gimbap, and raw beef. Famous from Netflix's Street Food.

Afternoon

Myeongdong

Shopper's paradise — K-beauty stores, street food stalls, fashion. Great for cosmetics and snacks.

Evening

Han River Picnic

Pick up convenience store chicken and head to Yeouido Hangang Park for a classic Seoul river evening.

5

Day Trip

Option A · Full Day

DMZ Tour

Guided tour of the Demilitarized Zone — Imjingak, the 3rd Infiltration Tunnel, Dora Observatory. Book in advance.

Option B · Full Day

Suwon Hwaseong Fortress

UNESCO World Heritage fortress (1796), 1 hour by train. Walk the full 5.7 km wall for great views.

Daily Itinerary

Arrival Day

Check-in & French Concession

Evening

Xintiandi

Upscale entertainment district in restored Shikumen stone-gate houses — great for dinner and drinks.

Stop 4 of 5

Xi'an

May 30 – Jun 2 · 2 nights

🏺

Daily Itinerary

1

Arrival Evening

Afternoon

Bell Tower Area

Your hotel is right by the Bell Tower — the geographic center of ancient Xi'an. A great landmark to orient yourself.

Evening

Muslim Quarter (Huimin Jie)

One of China's best food streets — try roujiamo (Chinese burger), biangbiang noodles, lamb skewers. Visit the Great Mosque.

2

Terracotta Army

Full Day — Go Early!

Terracotta Warriors & Horses

UNESCO World Heritage Site — 8,000+ life-size warriors buried with Emperor Qin Shi Huang. Three pits to explore. 1 hour from city. Truly unmissable — one of the world's greatest archaeological wonders.

Evening

Xi'an City Wall

Rent a bike and cycle the complete 14 km Ming Dynasty city wall at sunset. One of China's most complete ancient walls.

3

Mount Huashan

Full Day

Huashan Sacred Mountain

One of China's Five Sacred Taoist Mountains. The famous "Plank Walk in the Sky" is an iconic cliff-side trail. Take the cable car up, hike between the five peaks. 1.5 hours from Xi'an by bullet train.

Stop 5 of 5

Beijing

Jun 2 – 5 · 4 nights

🏯

Daily Itinerary

1

Arrival & Wangfujing

Afternoon

Check-in & Wangfujing Street

Beijing's famous shopping street — right next to your hotel. Try scorpion-on-a-stick at Donghuamen Night Market.

Evening

Tiananmen Square at Dusk

A short walk from the hotel — see the vast square lit up at dusk and admire the scale of this historic plaza.

2

Forbidden City

Morning — Book Tickets Online!

Tiananmen Square & Forbidden City

World's largest imperial palace — 980 buildings, 8,704 rooms. Allow 3+ hours. Pre-book on Palace Museum website — essential, sells out fast.

Afternoon

Jingshan Park

Hill directly behind the Forbidden City — climb for the best aerial view of the entire palace complex.

Evening

Hutong Dinner

Explore Nanluoguxiang or Wudaoying Hutong for traditional Beijing cuisine in the ancient alleyways.

3

The Great Wall

Full Day

Mutianyu Great Wall

Most scenic and least-crowded restored section. Optional toboggan ride down! ~1.5 hours from Beijing. Spend 3+ hours walking the wall between watchtowers.

Evening

Peking Duck Dinner

You've earned it! Try Quanjude or Da Dong — crispy skin, thin pancakes, hoisin sauce. Beijing's most iconic dish.

4

Last Day & Departure

✈️ Flight home at 19:50 — head to PEK airport by 17:00

Morning

Summer Palace

UNESCO imperial garden on Kunming Lake. Stroll the Long Corridor, rent a rowboat, visit the Marble Boat.

Afternoon

Last Hutong Walk

A final wander through the ancient alleyways — tea house, street food, last souvenirs.

17:00

Head to PEK Airport
